⭐ Fun Facts

🔴 Saka grew up supporting Arsenal and joined the club's academy when he was just 7 years old — the same age as his shirt number!

🥇 Saka won the FA Cup with Arsenal in 2019/20 and has since become the heartbeat of the team alongside captain Martin Ødegaard.
